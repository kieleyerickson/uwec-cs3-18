Palindromes

A palindrome (/ˈpæl. ɪn. droʊm/) is a word, number, phrase, or other sequence of symbols that reads the same backwards as forwards, such as madam or racecar, the date "22/02/2022", or the sentence "A man, a plan, a canal – Panama".
